About Us

At Dharanam, we believe that lack of resources shouldn’t limit your impact.That’s why we offer zero-cost services to help NGOs thrive—so they can focus on creating real change.

🚀 Our Mission

To equip NGOs with essential services—design, development, marketing, and compliance—at no cost.

🤝 Our Vision

A future where every NGO has access to the tools they need to create meaningful, lasting change.

Events

Our Journey of Togetherness & Impact

Donate Now

Sadaqat (28th October 2023, Lucknow)

A gathering of over 50 NGOs working in grassroots education and women's empowerment. Sadaqat provided a full day focused on collaboration, strategy sharing, and digital onboarding support for all participating organizations.

Donate Now

Samarthan (12th January 2024, Delhi)

An awareness and fundraising event featuring panel discussions with impact investors and NGO leaders. Fifteen organizations pitched their innovative work and received immediate micro-funding support from engaged donors.

Donate Now

Roots & Reach (8th March 2024, Pune)

Focused on rural NGOs, this specialized event provided personalized tech and design assistance. Organizations received professional guidance as they created their first logos and launched websites published live during the event.
